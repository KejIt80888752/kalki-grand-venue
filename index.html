About Kalki

Nestled in the serene surroundings of Dodd Baylkere, Bengaluru, Kalki The Grand Venue stands as a symbol of grandeur and elegance. We are a premier banquet hall and wedding venue dedicated to making your most cherished moments unforgettable.

From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, our venue offers a perfect blend of modern amenities and warm hospitality. Our professional team ensures every detail is handled with precision and care.
